Gaming Highlights Weekly (GHL) Site Plan

Braxton Butler

WDD 131-01

Overview

Purpose

To showcase and highlight the best gaming news and moments from the past week.

Audience

Anyone interested in gaming news or highlights, primarily teens and young adults in the technology and gaming space.

Dynamic elements

- A dark/light mode toggle that switches CSS variables and persists user preference in localStorage.
- Click-to-reveal detailed highlight descriptions on the child page using event listeners
